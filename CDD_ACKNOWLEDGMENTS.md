# CDD Acknowledgments

CDD didn't emerge in a vacuum. The agentic engineering community on X has been sharing workflows, patterns, and hard-won lessons openly. This methodology was shaped by watching, reading, and building on ideas from many people.

## Specific Credits

- [Jamon Holmgren](https://x.com/jamonholmgren) - The Night Shift pattern. Queue plans during the day, execute overnight, review stacked PRs in the morning.
- [Matt Pocock](https://x.com/mattpocockuk) - Agentic workflows and patterns for working effectively with AI coding agents.
- [Mac Martine](https://x.com/saasmakermac) - Ralph Wiggum prompting ecosystem and community-driven prompt engineering for agent-driven development.

## Community

Many others shared ideas on X that influenced how CDD works.
